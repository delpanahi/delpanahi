### Highlights of My Projects ###

- `Color Buddy`:
  In Oct. 2025, I participated in the EmpowHER 2025 week-long hackathon. My teammates and I chose to create an inclusive software tool called Color Buddy that allows designers to understand what their designs may look like to people with various types of color blindness. Through Color Buddy, we specifically show designers what their chosen colors look like to people who have protanopia, deuteranopia, or tritanopia. I played a key role in the development of this tool and was able to expand on my skills in JavaScript and Python.
  
- `SWE Website`:
  In Jan. 2025, I took on an executive board role (Publicity Chair) for The Society of Women Engineers at Clarkson University. The role's original responsibilities focused on creating engaging social media posts to promote our chapter, but I realized I wanted to take it a step further! I volunteered to create a website for Clarkson's Society of Women Engineers from scratch – since then, I have learned so much about frontend development with HTML/CSS and JavaScript!
